# Storytelling

  • CardBoard — Story-mapping and shared card analysis.

  • Earth Journalism Network — Environmental journalism and storytelling — their Resources Page is really great.

  • Outside the Lens — Digital media non-profit with a focus empowering youth through media creation.

  • Video4Change — Video activists and those looking to use video for social change.

  • Witness — Video activists, trainers and their allies.

  • PowToon — Animated cartoon videos.

  • Storify — Tools to create the best evergreen and live blog stories, uniting traditional storytelling with engaged audiences.

  • Adobe Spark — Fast way to tell stories with graphics, text, videos, and animations.

  • Ignite — Series of speaking events in 100+ cities designed to have each person share something innovative or inspiring in just five minutes.

  • Small World News — Supporting journalists and activists in under-served communities around the world tell compelling stories using mobile media tools and how to use them.

  • Visualising Information for Advocacy — Website for a book about how advocates and activists use visual elements in their campaigns.

  • [Esri Story Maps](organization, data, data collection, monitoring & evaluation) — Combine authoritative maps with narrative text, images, and multimedia content. They make it easy to harness the power of maps.

  • PechaKucha — Site for presentation format where you show 20 images, each for 20 seconds.
