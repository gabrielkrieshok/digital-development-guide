# Video

  • Live EDU — Videos of real-time coding.

  • Earth Journalism Network — Environmental journalism and storytelling — their Resources Page is really great.

  • Video4Change — Video activists and those looking to use video for social change.

  • Witness — Video activists, trainers and their allies.

  • Coverr — Free stock videos for your homepage.

  • Mazwai — Free stock video snippets.

  • Pexels Videos — Free stock videos.

  • Save From — Save videos from the Internet.

  • Videvo — Free stock motion graphics and video.

  • Vimeo — Free HD stock footage.

  • WeVideo — Online video editor for web, mobile, Windows & MacOS.

  • Audio Library — Browse and download free music and sound effects.

  • Adobe Spark — Fast way to tell stories with graphics, text, videos, and animations.

  • Ignite — Series of speaking events in 100+ cities designed to have each person share something innovative or inspiring in just five minutes.

  • Small World News — Supporting journalists and activists in under-served communities around the world tell compelling stories using mobile media tools and how to use them.

  • Visualising Information for Advocacy — Website for a book about how advocates and activists use visual elements in their campaigns.

  • PSDTUTS — Free tutorials in design and illustration, 3D, motion graphics, photography, and audio.

  • Lightworks — Free video-editing for uploading to YouTube.

  • VLC Player — Cross-platform multimedia player and framework that plays most multimedia files as well as DVDs, Audio CDs, VCDs, and various streaming protocols.

  • Integrating Low-cost Video into Agricultural Development Projects — Video toolkit for agricultural practices.
