# Collaboration

  • appear.in — Browser-based video conferencing.

  • BlueJeans Conferencing — Video-conferencing and screen-sharing — works well with faulty connections.

  • CardBoard — Story-mapping and shared card analysis.

  • Invision — Prototyping, collaboration & workflow platform.

  • RealtimeBoard — Online whiteboard & online collaboration tool.

  • Red Pen — Fast deliverable feedback.

  • VSee Messenger — Low-bandwidth video conferencing.

  • Gliffy — Collaborative diagrams — flow charts, log frames, etc.

  • Skype — Cross-platform messaging, able to use an account (with money added) to send/receive calls from traditional phone lines. Available on iOS, Android, Blackberry, and Windows Phone.

  • WhatsApp — Cross-platform mobile messaging app which allows you to exchange messages without having to pay for SMS.

  • Adium (MacOS) — Chat client for MacOS systems that supports AIM, Bonjour, Google Talk, ICQ, IRC, Yahoo!, and others.

  • EtherPad — Customizable online editor providing collaborative editing in really real-time.

  • Pidgin (Windows) — Chat client for PCs that supports AIM, Bonjour, Google Talk, ICQ, IRC, Yahoo!, and others.
